Our Story

Alcyone started as a senior thesis of Eirini Mitsa and Konstantinos Patsaras, when the goal was to design a prototype for a system service that offers localization and detection of objects defined by the user. Thesis supervisor is Stamelos Ioannis, professor at School of Informatics, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki.

Since successful completion of that project, Alcyone has grown and plans to offer an advance system for segmentation and detection of sea pollution incidents. In addition our system is capable of offering seaport surveillance and open sea monitoring for illegal ship activities.
